Our Team

We are seasoned brokers ready to serve you in many diverse areas of real estate and business brokerage, putting our more than 45 years of combined experience to work for your business success.

We can assist you in English, Spanish or Russian, and indeed, if you click the upper right area of our website, you can choose to read our site in one of those languages.

Our brokerage team includes:

Mathew Abraham

Mathew is the owner and founder of the company, which has been in operation since 1980. Over the last 25 years, Matthew has brokered hundreds of commercial real estate and business opportunity transactions. His expertise in commercial real estate spans retail, office, industrial and land throughout the Denver metro area including representing the City of Westminster in the acquisition of a large tract of land at 120th and Huron for open space.

His portfolio includes sales of shopping centers, churches, day care centers, office buildings and scores of retail sales and leases. Mathew's business brokerage experience extends from liquor stores to day care centers, including convenience stores, automobile repair and recycling sites, dry cleaners, hotels, motels, existing gas stations and new gas station development, and scores of restaurants as well as franchises such as 7-Eleven and Subway. He is especially adept at the sales and acquisition of liquor stores - both existing and new --and the myriad of challenges they entail such as liquor license applications and transfers, lease assignments, petitioning, zoning issues, etc. Matthew recently negotiated one of the largest liquor store deals in Colorado (20,000 square feet) representing the buyer; and sold the popular Bonnie Brae Liquor store in a transaction where he represented the seller.

Because of his experience as a commercial real estate broker, Mathew can expertly advise his clients about the real estate side of any transaction, whether the business purchase involves buying, leasing, negotiating new terms or lease assignments. Mathew also brings expertise in evaluating businesses to the table, which is a significant asset in advising his clients on pricing when either buying or selling a business, as well as maximizing value on the selling side.

Mathew is known for his integrity, and looking out for the best interests and well-being of his clients. He won't let anyone buy a business if he isn't convinced that it's a good deal or a good fit for the client, whether financially, lifestyle-wise or presents a solid opportunity for long-term success.

Please visit our Testimonials page to read what customers and clients say about their experience of working with Matthew, for example "sold our liquor store in record time" "always got back to us instantly," "depth of character," "a happy conclusion," "keen understanding," "integrity and fairness" "knowledge and skill" "the best brokerage company when buying or selling a business," "super-fast," "everything transparent," "genuine."

Please call or email Mathew today to explore how he can be of service to you for your commercial real estate or business opportunity brokerage needs: mabraham@msn.com or 303-359-7868 (cell phone).
