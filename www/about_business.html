Why Work With Us?

Eagle Business Brokers specializes in commercial real estate transactions and has built a niche in the buying and selling of business opportunities. We make it easy for our clients to acquire a new business or sell an existing business. In addition we can help in all the endeavors of starting a new business, strategizing for expansion, improving profitability, mergers and dispositions.

Most business owners are committed to being successful, and know the ins and outs of their business. However when it comes to acquisition or transition, it demands a different set of experience, which most are not accustomed to. That is where Eagle Business Brokers delivers real value. We will walk you through step-by-step to achieve your goal.

Our Approach

At Eagle Business Brokers we believe we can make a difference in your life, and we can make your business better. As specialists we take this philosophy to the next level.

Whether you are buying or selling a business or real estate, it is a complicated transaction and chances are this is an unfamiliar experience for you. The team at Eagle Business Brokers understands that – and that is why we have developed a system to ensure success.

Whether you are an experienced operator or considering your first transaction, we want you to succeed, and now how to make that happen. It’s not just paying attention to the details, it’s much more. As specialists we have vast and in depth experience, a unique skill set and knowledge base. This valuable expertise makes your transaction more efficient and ensures the best outcomes, but more important, it helps you achieve your long- term goals.

Marketing Your Property and/or Business Opportunity

When selling or leasing your commercial real estate property or business, we will implement proven tools to effectively market your property. We utilize the perfect combination of professionally-designed, industry- proven and strategically-timed marketing materials to bring our clients the best results.

The way we market our listing

  • A marketing brochure of your listing
  • A customized direct mailing
  • Email broadcast to investors, owners and brokers
